The real cost

What does a broken data governance framework cost?

Governance gaps do not announce themselves. They surface in audits, board meetings, and the incidents your team finds out about in standup.

Policies that exist on paper

Access rules live in spreadsheets. No enforcement layer means no audit trail and no accountability when data moves.

Quality failures with no owner

Dashboards break. Pipelines pass. No one knows which dataset failed or who is responsible for fixing it.

Lineage gaps at the worst moment

A compliance question arrives. You cannot trace where the data came from or who touched it last.

Governance slows the data team

Manual tagging, ownership requests, and access reviews consume engineering hours that should go to building.

How DataHub solves it

A better way to enforce your governance framework

Four integrated capabilities that turn governance policy into automated, auditable enforcement across your entire data stack.

Access control

Enforce policies at the resource level

DataHub's policy engine applies fine-grained RBAC by resource, domain, tag, or owner. Every access decision is logged. Every policy change creates an audit event.

  • Resource-level, privilege-based, and actor-based policies
  • GraphQL and REST APIs for programmatic policy management
  • Full audit log: CreatePolicy, UpdatePolicy, DeletePolicy events
Data quality

Turn data contracts into quality gates

Define contracts for schema, freshness, volume, and custom SQL assertions. DataHub monitors them continuously and alerts your team before downstream consumers notice.

  • Assertion types: volume, freshness, schema, field-level, SQL
  • Data contracts with SLA, schema, and quality enforcement
  • Alerts routed to Slack or PagerDuty on contract violations
Lineage tracking

Trace every dataset to its source

Column-level lineage connects your warehouses, transformation layers, and BI tools into a single traceable graph. Impact analysis runs before a breaking change ships.

  • End-to-end lineage across datasets, jobs, and flows
  • Column-level lineage for Snowflake, BigQuery, dbt, and Looker
  • Upstream impact analysis before schema changes deploy
Data governance metrics

Measure governance health across every domain

Track ownership coverage, assertion pass rates, and policy adoption by domain or team. Governance metrics surface in dashboards your leads already use.

  • Ownership coverage tracked by domain and asset type
  • Assertion pass rates trended over time by dataset
  • Policy adoption reported by team and resource group
